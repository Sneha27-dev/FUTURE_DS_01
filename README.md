# Business Sales Performance Dashboard
## Future Interns – Data Science & Analytics | Task 1

## Overview
This project analyzes business sales data to identify revenue trends, 
top-selling products, category performance, and regional sales using Power BI.

## Dataset
- Source: Superstore Sales Dataset (Kaggle)
- Records: 9,994 rows
- Fields: Order Date, Product Name, Category, Region, Sales, Profit, Quantity

## Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop
- Microsoft Excel

## Dashboard Visuals
- Monthly Sales Trend (Line Chart)
- Top 10 Products by Sales (Bar Chart)
- Sales by Category (Donut Chart)
- Sales by Region (Bar Chart)
- Interactive Year Slicer

## Key Insights
1. West region leads in total revenue across all years
2. Technology category contributes the highest sales (36.4%)
3. Canon imageCLASS is the top selling product
4. South region has the lowest sales performance
5. Sales peak consistently in Q4 every year

## Recommendations
1. Increase marketing budget in Q4 to maximize peak season revenue
2. Focus on improving sales strategies in South region
3. Expand Technology product range as it drives most revenue
